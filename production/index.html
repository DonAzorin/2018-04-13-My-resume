My life

Some of the things that I like are the teaching of history, cycling and being stuck to the computer or the smartphone. One day, after having completed my bachelor's degree in history and being a professor for almost four years, I decided that I wanted more.

It was when I listened to the words of my friends and my degree project adviser. I started that path with doubts and fear as it was a new starting point in my life, far from what I had done for years. A night of cat gifs and Wikipedia took me from the article of the day to others who have no relationship other than a link to another article and another... And so I got to know the bootcamps and the world of the front end. I already knew what I wanted to do. And how was missing.

After finding a lot of motivation in articles that narrated how other people with careers so different to the programming like me, with more age and worse economic situations than those that I have lived, I decided to learn in some bootcamp and it was when I had the opportunity to learn for three months my first steps as a web developer at Centraal Academy.
